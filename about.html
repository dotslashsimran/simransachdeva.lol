Hello! I'm Simran, Simran Sachdeva. Currently in my fourth year of studying Computer Science Engineering, with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning.

My area of expertise is Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), which I find fascinating and challenging. I am recognized as a top voice in machine learning on LinkedIn, where I share insights, resources, and discussions with a community passionate about AI. Additionally, I have had the privilege of mentoring over 750 students, helping them navigate their learning journeys in tech, which has been incredibly rewarding.

On the creative side, I enjoy web and graphic design, merging technology with artistry to create visually engaging and user-friendly experiences. I also maintain a Substack where I share my thoughts and insights, though I admit I update it infrequently.

Reading is another passion of mine, and I love exploring new books and ideas. Additionally, I enjoy traveling and discovering new places and cultures. (and taking semi-cool pictures)

I believe in balancing work and leisure, and I always look forward to engaging conversations and learning from others. :)
